TVS Spaces was born amidst global change, when we recognized that Egypt's professionals deserved inspiring spaces that blend local culture with global standards. Starting with a single location in Masr El Gedida, we created a haven where ideas could flourish.

Today

We've grown into a thriving community hub where Cairo's brightest minds connect, collaborate, and create. Our spaces reflect the vibrant spirit of Egypt while providing world-class amenities that empower our members to achieve their greatest potential.
